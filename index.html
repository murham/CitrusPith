Hi.

I'm happy you found me! My name is Ian, and I work as a freelance artist. I do character designs from head to chest, usually a JPG, PDF, or PNG file at 300 DPI resolution. During the design process, I will message you after these two steps: sketching, and after coloring + shading. Revisions can be requested then - I'll be happy to adjust to you.

Things You Should Know.

  1. I would like a thorough description of the character you want me to draw to guarantee your satisfaction of the product.
  2. The character doesn't have to be human; I can draw nonhuman characters, too.
  3. I'm very bad at drawing horses.
